A. Alert-First

Top: Live critical alerts across all outlets. Below: Status cards (color-coded) per outlet, click to expand items.

Pros

  • Instant attention to critical issues
  • Alerts first, overview second
  • Monitor tile

Cons

  • Need to drill-down for full picture
  • Less overview at-a-glance

B. Outlet Cards

Grid/list of cards: Each outlet shows summary (status, item count at-risk, opname status). Click card to expand full item detail.

Pros

  • Good balance: overview + drillable
  • Responsive (tablet + monitor)
  • Visual grouping per outlet

Cons

  • May need scrolling on many outlets

C. Table + Tabs

Tabs for different views: Overview (all items), Alerts (below-threshold), Compliance (opname status). Table shows Outlet | Item | Qty/Threshold | Status.

Pros

  • Data-dense, comprehensive
  • Easy filtering/sorting
  • Good for compliance audit

Cons

  • Less "at-a-glance" visual
  • May feel busy on monitor

💡 Selection note: We can also combine approaches (e.g., A + C: alerts on top, tabs below). Pick what resonates with how you naturally scan information.
